MAXXReplay Cricket ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the MAXXReplay Cricket mobile application (the "App").

1. Information We Collect

MAXXReplay Cricket is designed to operate with minimal data collection.

a) Information You Provide

  • No account creation is required
  • We do not collect names, email addresses, or personal identifiers

b) Video & Media Data

  • Video recordings are created only when initiated by the user
  • All footage remains on your device unless exported by you
  • No video is uploaded or stored on external servers

c) Device Information

Basic device and operating system data may be processed by Apple for App Store analytics. We do not access or store this data.

2. How We Use Information

The App uses information solely to enable replay functionality, improve stability, and meet App Store requirements.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share personal data. All recorded footage remains under the user’s control.

4. Third-Party Services

The App relies only on Apple iOS system services and the App Store. No advertising or tracking SDKs are used.

5. Data Security

Users are responsible for securing their devices and managing any shared or exported content.

6. Children’s Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect data from children under 13 and is intended for supervised club use.

7. Policy Updates

This policy may be updated periodically. Updates will be posted within the App or on this website.
